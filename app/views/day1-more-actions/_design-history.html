Adding UPLs 8 and 10 to the application journey Design history

Why we are adding UPL8 and UPL10

The team chose SFI actions UPL8 and UPL10 to add next to the private beta (in addition to the original 4 actions: UPL 1 to 3, and CMOR1). UPL8 and UPL10 are both actions which cover shepherding livestock on moorland.

The reason for adding more actions is because we need to scale up the private beta. The service will ultimately need to include many more actions, so this is only the first step.

The reason for selecting UPL8 and 10 specifically was because these are considered to be operationally and technically straightforward actions which will not add any complexity to the 4 existing actions. UPL8 and 10 are also expected to continue being available under SFI26 (unlike some other UPL actions).

Scope

Adding UPL8 and UPL10 does not affect the case working journey and has almost no operational or technical impact in terms of our private beta service. The team assumed there are no specific user needs or pain points for UPL8 and 10 that could affect design.

The only operational changes which adding UPL8 and 10 will need are to the private beta guidance documents which users agree to.

Design

We added radio buttons and content for the 2 new actions to the screen where the user selects their actions for a land parcel.

The compatibility rules for these actions, as provided by the Rural Payments Agency (see sheet 2 of this spreadsheet), is that a farmer can do any one of UPL1 to UPL3, plus either UPL8 or UPL10, on the same parcel of land. We reflected this rule on the actions selection page of the prototype.

We also updated the loop to add another action, the basket page, and the review agreement offer page to add UPL8 and UPL10.

We used the latest known payment rates for SFI26 and also updated the payment rates for the existing actions in line with SFI26, as these will be uplifted for our private beta users.

CMOR1 will be removed from SFI26 but will remain in our private beta so we did not need to remove it.

We decided not to add pagination where there are many actions or parcels to display based on past UR which told us that farmers would prefer a long page to separate pages.

What is still to do

We were not able to amend the documents linked on the submit page as this is currently still in hand with policy and legal. These documents (with the possible exception of the terms and conditions), and the underlying policy definition document, all need to be updated for UPL8 and UPL10, and indeed any other actions which we add. This is because these documents were simplified and limited to only covering the first 4 actions of private beta and explicitly do not apply to any other actions.

Content design will be needed to help update these documents and links and the version with UPL8 and UPL10 cannot legally be used with real private beta users until this is completed.

Next steps

The simplicity of adding UPL8 and 10 should not be taken as an indication that adding any actions will be simple.
The next iteration will be to explore adding more actions, which we expect to be much more complex.

The current private beta design is not scalable because it is only based on a few compatible actions. Once other kinds of actions are added, for example linear or incompatible actions, there will be more technical and operational complications. It will require significantly more design exploration to create a scalable service to which any kind of action can be added.
