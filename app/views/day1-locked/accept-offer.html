Accept your agreement offer

Your agreement consists will consist of the:

  • agreement document
  • terms and conditions (opens in new tab)
  • actions you have selected

By accepting this offer, you confirm on your own behalf or, where applicable, on behalf of the agreement holder, that:

  • you will comply with the obligations under the your agreement
  • the information you gave and the declarations you made in your application remain true and accurate to the best of your knowledge and belief
  • you have full capacity and authority to enter into the agreement
  • you are not aware of any circumstances which would render you ineligible or otherwise prevent you from complying with your obligation under the agreement
  • your obligations under the agreement do not and will not duplicate or conflict in whole or in part with any other legal or contractual obligations you are subject to
  • you will at all times comply with all relevant law (as defined in the terms and conditions) in the performance of your obligations under the agreement

Check the terms and conditions to understand what can happen if you deliberately give wrong or incomplete information, or do not report changes as soon as possible.

In addition to the confirmations above, if you are accepting this offer on behalf of the agreement holder, you confirm you are duly authorised and have the capacity to:

  • accept this agreement on the agreement holder’s behalf
  • enter into the agreement on the agreement holder’s behalf
