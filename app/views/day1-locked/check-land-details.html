Confirm your land details are up to date

You confirm you have checked your digital maps are up to date and show:

  • all the land parcels you want to include in your application
  • the correct total area (in hectares) for each land parcel
  • the correct land covers for each land parcel, for example, arable land, permanent grassland, permanent crops or relevant non-agricultural land cover

You confirm you have declared the correct land use codes for each land parcel in the Rural Payments service.

If your land details are not up to date:

  • you may not be able to apply for the actions you select
  • the total area of land available to enter into an action may not be accurate
