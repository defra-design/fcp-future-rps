Land with historic or archaeological features

If you apply for SFI actions on land with historic or archaeological features, you must get an Historic Environment Farm Environment Record (HEFER) from Historic England.

You must do this before you do your selected SFI actions on this land.

Read the guidance on land with historic or archaeological features to find out what a HEFER is and how to request one.

If you are not applying for SFI actions on land with historic or archaeological features, answer ‘yes’ to continue your application.
