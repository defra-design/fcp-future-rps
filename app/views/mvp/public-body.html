Public bodies

If you're a public body, you can apply for an SFI agreement. But you will not be paid for activities you're required to carry out as part of your statutory duties. You will not be paid for activities that are already funded by other sources either.

Read the guidance about the eligibility of public bodies.

If you're not a public body, or you're not a tenant on land owned by a public body, answer 'yes' to continue your application.

Do the actions you want to apply for fall outside of your statutory duties, and can you confirm they are not already funded by other sources?
