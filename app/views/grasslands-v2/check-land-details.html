Before you continue with this application, check the land details on your digital maps are up to date. If they are not, this may prevent you from applying for the SFI actions you select. Read the guidance on how to check your digital maps.

Check that your digital maps show:

  • all the land parcels you want to include in this application
  • the correct total area (in hectares) for each land parcel
