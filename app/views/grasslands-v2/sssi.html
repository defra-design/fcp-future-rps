Sites of special scientific interest

You can apply for SFI actions on land that's a site of special scientific interest (SSSI) if it's eligible for the action. You can check this for each action in the 'Find Funding for land or farms' tool.

Before you do SFI actions on SSSI land, you must get SSSI consent from Natural England.

Read the SFI scheme information on SSSI consent and how to get it.

If you do not want to apply for SFI actions on SSSI land, select 'yes' to continue with your application.
