Land with historic or archaeological features

You can apply for SFI actions on land with historic or archaeological features if it's eligible for the action. You can check this for each action in the 'Find funding for land or farms' tool.

Before you do SFI actions on land with historic or archaeological features, you must get an SFI Historic Environment Farm Environment Record (HEFER) from Historic England.

Read the SFI scheme information on SFI HEFERs and how to get one.

If you do not want to apply for SFI actions on land with historic or archaeological features, select 'yes' to continue with your application.
