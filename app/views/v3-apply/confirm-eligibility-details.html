Confirm you will be eligible

You confirm you will:

  • have management control of the land you are including in your application for the duration of the actions you select
  • check your land will remain eligible for the duration of the actions you select
  • get an Historic Environment Farm Environment Record (HEFER) from Historic England if you apply for actions on land with historic or archaeological features - you must not start doing these actions until you have your HEFER
  • get site of special scientific interest (SSSI) consent from Natural England if you select to do actions on SSSI land - you must not start doing these actions until you have your SSSI consent
  • check if you can do your selected actions on your land if you have land with a conditional exemption from inheritance tax or maintenance fund
  • not apply for actions that include activities you are legally required to carry out or are already funded by other sources - this applies to public bodies
