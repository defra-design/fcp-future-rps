Liquid fertiliser applicator for seed drills/planters 6m (FETF212)

Liquid fertiliser applicator for seed drills, with minimum 6m drilling or planting width.

Grant amount per item: £11,031.00

Maximum quantity: 33

Expected average cost of item: £18,385.00

Percentage paid towards item: 60%

Item score: 71

How items are scored

Applications for productivity items are scored on:

  • productivity benefit
  • environmental benefit
  • level of adoption by the industry

Each productivity item has a score up to 100.

The RPA will give your application a score by:

  • adding together the scores for each item in your application (if you apply for more than one of the same item, the item will only be scored once)
  • dividing this number by the number of items in your application (if you apply for more than one of the same item, the item will only be counted once)

Items must:

  • be a liquid fertiliser applicator for seed drills
  • incorporate the solution into the soil as part of the drilling or planting process
  • have a minimum 6m drilling or planting width
  • have a fill tank
  • have a hydraulic powered pump
  • have a drill mounted liquid distribution manifold
  • have pressure regulation
  • have a nozzle and pump flush system
  • apply the solution at different rates using built in calibration
  • have a system to prevent nozzles from blocking or a liquid blockage monitoring system

Applicators that use surface application of liquid fertiliser or liquid chemicals such as herbicides and fungicides are not eligible.

Serial number: You must provide this item’s serial number when you submit your claim for payment.
