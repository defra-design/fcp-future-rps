Version 1 (June/July 2025) Agreements

An initial prototype to show how private beta day 1 will work for an external user.

The scenario is that a user has previously been invited to join the private beta. As part of that process all their details and land information were checked to ensure that everything was correct and up to date prior to starting an application.

They have gone through the process of applying for actions CMOR1, UPL3, UPL4, SPM5 and UPL10 against land parcel SO3757 3159 and have selected to submit the application.

The flow picks up from that point, by showing them their offer to review and accept, before then being able to view their agreement.
